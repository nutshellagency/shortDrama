ShortDrama – Executive Summary & Pitch Deck
1. Executive Summary
ShortDrama is a mobile-first, cross-platform OTT application focused on short-form, episodic
vertical video dramas (9:16) designed for Indian and Pakistani audiences. The platform delivers
culturally relevant Hindi and Urdu content in bingeable episodes of 1–3 minutes, optimized for high
engagement and monetization.
Inspired by the success of ReelShort, ShortDrama differentiates itself through regional storytelling,
cost-effective infrastructure, and an AI-powered content processing pipeline that converts traditional
video into vertical, mobile-native formats.
2. Problem
Existing OTT platforms are long-form, data-heavy, and not optimized for mobile-first consumption
habits in South Asia. Short-form platforms lack structured storytelling and monetization depth.
3. Solution
ShortDrama offers vertically formatted episodic stories with unlock mechanics, localized pricing,
and AI-assisted content ingestion, making premium drama accessible, addictive, and scalable.
4. Target Market
Primary users are 16–35-year-old mobile-first viewers in India and Pakistan who consume short
video content daily. The platform is language-first (Hindi/Urdu) and culturally aligned.
5. Product Highlights
- Vertical episodic dramas (1–3 min) - Swipe-based immersive viewing - Ad + coin-based unlocking
- AI-powered vertical video conversion - Scalable, cost-efficient backend
6. Monetization
Revenue is generated via rewarded ads, in-app coin purchases, subscriptions (phase 2), and
sponsored original series.
7. Technology & Scalability


=== PAGE 2 ===
Built using Flutter, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and CDN-backed video delivery. AI media workers are
decoupled for independent scaling.
8. Vision
To become the leading short-drama OTT platform for South Asia, enabling studios and creators to
monetize storytelling in a mobile-native format.
